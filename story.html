Who I am in a few lines

I'm a developer born in 2007 who loves building things, from Minecraft servers to web applications. I'm passionate about automation, Linux, and creating practical solutions.

This page tells the story of my journey into tech, how I've grown as a developer, and what I'm working on right now. I hope you enjoy learning about my path.

My Journey

2023

Built my first PC

Built my first PC, which sparked my interest in hardware and software. This experience led me to explore the world of technology and programming, setting the foundation for my future projects and learning journey.

2024

Minecraft Servers & Web Development

Started developing Minecraft game servers on commission, creating custom gameplay experiences. During this period, I also began learning web development to expand my skills and build better digital solutions. This marked the beginning of my full-stack journey.

2025

Linux & Programming Languages

Discovered the world of Linux and started learning Python for scripting and automation. Also began studying SQL through school, understanding databases and data management. These skills opened new possibilities for backend development and system administration.

Late 2025 - Early 2026

Home Server & NAS

Built my own home server with NAS functionality. This project deepened my understanding of Linux, networking, and system administration. It became the foundation for running my own infrastructure and hosting personal projects.

2026

Diploma & AI Home Automation

In 2026 I will graduate with a diploma in Computer Science and Telecommunications from ITIS Magistri Cumacini. At the same time, I'm developing an AI automation bot that controls and automates my homeserver, using Raspberry Pi and Zeroclaw.
